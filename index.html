Welcome to Mambo's

Mambo's Bar & Restaurant, located in Kapparis, Cyprus, has been serving delicious Mediterranean cuisine since 1998. With over 200 glowing reviews and a 4.6-star rating, we are committed to offering an unforgettable dining experience.

Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, a fun night out with friends, or a family gathering, we guarantee an extraordinary experience with exceptional food and service.
